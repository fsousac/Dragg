# Code of Conduct

Dragg is an open-source project. We want this community to be welcoming, respectful, and practical.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent when discussing bugs, design, and implementation choices.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect maintainers' time by providing clear context and reproduction steps.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private information
- Spam or unrelated promotion
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this code, contact the maintainer privately. Do not escalate interpersonal or security-sensitive issues through public threads.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block users who violate this code.
